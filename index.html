About me

Hi, I'm Matej Jelić, an aspiring game developer based in Vukovar, Croatia. My love for games started at a young age, and I've been captivated by the immersive experiences they offer ever since. I'm passionate about creating games that transport players to new worlds and challenge them to explore, learn, and grow.

As a self-taught developer, I've spent countless hours learning about game development and exploring various games. With my strong technical skills, creativity, and drive to succeed, I'm committed to making my mark in the world of game development.
